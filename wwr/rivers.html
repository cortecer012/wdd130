Rivers

This page is to give information about the rivers that the company runs. Discover everything about the different rivers we have for you. Find detailed information about each location and its rapids. Take a look at the complete guide to explore each river in every place. We have everything you need to know about the rivers so you can explore their wildlife and rapids.

SALMON RIVER, IDAHO

The Salmon River is an impressive place to observe wildlife and enjoy class I-IV rapids. The best time to visit is during the summer. Rapids classification: I-IV. Race duration: variable.

Book Now

GRAND CANYON, ARIZONA

The Grand Canyon is a must-visit destination for world-class whitewater rafting and breathtaking scenery. The unique river classification scale ranges from I to X, with most people familiar with the regular river classification scale of I-V. Rapid classification: I-X. Race duration: variable.

Book Now

DESOLATION AND GREY CANYON, UTAH

Desolation and Grey Canyon is a 6-day trip through canyons in the center of Utah that features beautiful desert landscapes and great whitewater opportunities. Rapids classification: II/III. Race duration: 6 days.
